BIBIMBAP

Prep Time: 30 Minutes

Cook Time: 1 Hour

Total Time: 1 Hour 30 Minutes

Yields: 2 To 3 Servings

Bibimbap is one of my favorite Korean dishes, combining so many flavors and textures into one delicious bowl. With tender marinated bulgogi, a variety of seasoned vegetables, and a spicy gochujang sauce, each bite is a delightful mix of savory, sweet, and spicy. Topped with a fried egg and served over sizzling jasmine rice, this dish is the definition of COMFORT!
